Few words about me

Seemingly an ongoing student, globetrotter and by the way a mother.
Lived on few continents, among various cultures. Spectacularily influenced by early longer term stay in Detroit, USA, where living with my god mother and her family gave me the opportunity to live a life of a typical teenager from the area, which enabled me to devour English language. Upon return to my homeland Poland I realised that thanks to this experience I have opened up a gate to foreign languages in my brain, this resulting in my late diploma thesis titled "Mind mapping in aquisition of foreign languages". I'm fluent in: German, English oraz Italian.
On daily basis I'm busy with implementing knowledge to the brains of my own children: a 5 and 12yro. Living my own way wearing jeand and The Doors t-shirt with cat on my lap.
Lately I've engaged to frontend being my current challenge.

Education

Generally I've been busy with quite a few occupations, but the Uni I've finished made me an English philologist. Currently working as order manager yet as the time goes on and I begin to cherish more privacy in my life, the frontend seems to be it for the future.
